# TASTE-SKILL V1.0
## Editorial Luxury Archetype

### 1. CORE PRINCIPLES
- **Variance (8/10)**: High layout unpredictability. Asymmetric grids. Staggered rhythms. No 3-column equal cards.
- **Motion (6/10)**: Fluid, eased transitions. Haptic feedback. Reveal-on-scroll.
- **Density (4/10)**: High negative space. Breathable margins. Minimalist content per fold.
- **Complexity (5/10)**: Simple interactions with hidden depth (quick add on hover).

### 2. ANTI-PATTERNS (Hard Constraints)
- NO Inter, Roboto, or Arial.
- NO pure black (#000000).
- NO generic shadows.
- NO purple/neon AI gradients.
- NO centered heroes (unless variance < 4).
- NO "3 equal box" service grids.

### 3. TYPOGRAPHY PAIRING
- **Display**: Instrument Serif (Italicized for emphasis).
- **Body**: Outfit (Grotesque, high legibility).
- **Meta/UI**: JetBrains Mono (Precision and technicality).

### 4. COLOR SPEC
- Base: Canvas (#FDFBF7).
- Ink: Slate-Grey/Charcoal (#1a1714).
- Accent: Single amber/sand tone (#c4956a).
